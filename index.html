My Story

Every birth deserves to be supported.

I became a doula after the birth of my second child, when I experienced firsthand the difference compassionate, continuous support can make. That experience changed the trajectory of my life and set me on a path to hold space for other families during one of life's most profound transitions.

My approach is rooted in evidence-based care, deep listening, and the belief that every birthing person already has the wisdom they need — sometimes they just need someone in their corner reminding them of that. Whether you're planning a home birth, a hospital birth, or a cesarean, I'm here to support your choices without judgment.

I serve families in the greater Portland, Oregon area and offer virtual support for those further afield.

Services

Birth Doula Support

  • Two prenatal visits to build our relationship and discuss your birth preferences
  • On-call support from 37 weeks
  • Continuous labor & delivery support

  • One postpartum follow-up visit

Postpartum Doula Support

  • Daytime or overnight support
  • Newborn care guidance and feeding support
  • Light meal preparation
  • Emotional support and active listening

  • Local resource referrals

Childbirth Education
